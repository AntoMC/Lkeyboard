Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2025

At Liquid Keyboard, we recognize that your privacy is of the utmost importance, especially for a tool used to input text. This document outlines our commitment to protecting your information.

Important: This keyboard DOES NOT collect, DOES NOT store, and DOES NOT transmit any of your keystrokes. This includes passwords, private messages, bank details, or emails.

1. Data Collection & Processing

Our application is designed to operate entirely locally. We do not require account creation, and we do not request personal data for the keyboard or the Text Expander features to function.

  • Local Storage: Your custom layouts, snippets, and clipboard history are stored exclusively on your device's internal storage.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not use external servers to process your text or predictions.

2. System Permissions

To function as an Input Method Editor (IME), Liquid Keyboard requires standard Android permissions:

  • Input Method Service: Essential to display the keyboard across your system.
  • Vibration: Used only for haptic feedback during key presses.
  • Internet Access: Required strictly for loading advertisements (AdMob) within the settings screen.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements, which helps us keep the app free and maintained. AdMob may collect anonymous advertising identifiers to show relevant ads. You can review Google's privacy practices at Google's Official Privacy Policy.

4. Security Commitment

Because our core logic does not connect to the internet to process your typing, your sensitive data remains within the sandboxed environment of your device, protected by Android's security architecture.
