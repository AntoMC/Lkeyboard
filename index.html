🚀 Quick Start Guide

1. Setup Snippets

Save time with shortcuts. Go to Personalization > Text Expander and add your snippets. Type !html to expand a full boilerplate instantly.

2. Enable on Android

After installation, go to Settings > System > Languages > On-screen keyboard and toggle Liquid Keyboard to ON.

3. Master Corner Swipes

The "soul" of Liquid Keyboard. Swipe from the corners of any key to type symbols. No more layout switching for a ; or {.
